Smart Blood Donor & Emergency Healthcare Assistance System

A healthcare platform connecting blood donors, hospitals and patients during emergencies.

Project Overview

The Smart Blood Donor & Emergency Healthcare Assistance System helps patients quickly find blood donors and hospitals during emergency situations. The platform aims to reduce response time and improve healthcare accessibility.

Key Features

Blood Donor Registration
Donor Search By Blood Group
Hospital Bed Availability
Emergency Request Management
